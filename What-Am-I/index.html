What Am I?

A yes/no guessing game for the ESL classroom

Game Mode

Time Limit

Difficulty

How to Play

1

One student sits in front of the screen, facing the class. They cannot see the screen.

2

The class can see the word on the screen but cannot say it, spell it, or act it out.

3

The guesser asks yes or no questions to figure out what they are.

4

The class can only answer yes or no. When the guesser gets it right, click Correct!

💡 Starter Questions for the Guesser

Start broad, then get more specific:

Am I an animal? Am I a person? Am I a thing? Am I a food? Am I an action? Am I alive? Am I big? Am I found indoors? Am I found outdoors? Can people use me? Do I make noise? Am I in this classroom?
